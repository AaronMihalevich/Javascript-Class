About Me

My Name is Aaron Mihalevich

I enjoy many things in life. I have always had a strong passion for art and design but in a digital form. I gravitated to Photoshop at a young age and was mostly self taught. I was motivated by the exceptional power that the software gave me. So many features and functions to express my creativity. This led me to pursue a graphic design degree at OTC. After graduated I found a job in a windowless room with very little creative output. After only 9 months of this I quit my job and started selling outdoor gear and supplies at Ozark Adventures in Springfield. I've always had a strong passion for the outdoors and in the years leading up to this job I had become hooked on spending my free time exploring. I loved this job but the extreme discounts didn't hurt.

Home Town

I was born in Columbia, MO but grew up in Jefferson City, MO.

Free Time

I love watching movies and TV at night. In my free time I go paddling, camping, biking, climbing and other outdoor activities.
